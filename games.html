Lost, INC.

Jan. 2023

You got the job. Don't mess this up.

Lost, INC. is a game where you work for a lost and found company. Find items reported missing around the city and return them to their respective owners' doors. Do make sure to do your job well, or else your boss will not be happy.

Made for the 44th gm48, theme "Delivery". Used the game jam's leaderboards system for displaying best scores among players. This was also the project I was in charge for most stuff. Also everything almost blew up towards the end!

Spread a Smile

Apr. 2022

Collect candy around the streets, then head to some house to shoot "baddies" out of people's heads. Try getting the best score and number of candies in the world!

My team's best-placing game in gm48 to this day. The jam's theme was "Non-Violence", so we tried to come up with something that's borderline "violent", but not quite there.

People really liked the concept of the game, and felt very competitive fighting for the best score.

Coding, sound effects
Dantoma

Oct. 2021

The big evil ghost stole your potion recipes book! Explore a haunted library, fight ghosts with your three magic spells and take back what's yours!

Of all my jam games, Dantoma is my personal favorite. This was the first time I actually worked with a team on a game AND made a procedural generation system for the level (albeit a simple one). Made for the 40th gm48, theme "Discovery".

We liked this game so much, we're actually working on a full version of it! It's going slow, but it's going!

Coding, sound effects
Burning Down

Oct. 2021

The house is on fire! Get inside and save all people, using your extinguisher to put down fire and access other areas!

Burning Down was my first jam game ever. Other than the sound effects, everything was made all by myself. Made for LD49, theme "Unstable".

I joined this jam mostly for forcing myself into Unity. I'm glad I managed to put all of this together in under 72 hours. The game was praised for having that much content made by a first timer in such a short time!
